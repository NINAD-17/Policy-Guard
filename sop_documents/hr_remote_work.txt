1. PURPOSE & OBJECTIVE
   This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines remote work expectations, core 
   working hours, communication parameters, and expense reimbursement processes 
   for remote employees at PulseGuard. The goal is to maximize employee productivity,
   ensure business continuity, maintain data security, and facilitate structured 
   reimbursements.

2. SCOPE
   This procedure is applicable to all remote-first employees, hybrid workers,
   and contract staff working off-site within any department of PulseGuard 
   Technologies Inc. It applies across all geographical work zones.

3. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
   3.1. Remote Employee
        - Remains reachable and responsive during core working hours.
        - Sets up a private and secure home workspace.
        - Wears appropriate business attire during video calls.
   3.2. Team Manager
        - Conducts daily team check-ins (standups).
        - Monitors team communication response times.
        - Approves expense submissions for office upgrades.
   3.3. HR Coordinator
        - Audits remote workspace compliance profiles.
        - Process remote work applications and ergonomic gear requests.

4. DEFINITIONS AND REFERENCES
   - Core Working Hours: Mandatory daily windows where all remote personnel must
     be online and responsive.
   - Slack Latency: The maximum delay permitted for responding to direct text
     messages during working hours.
   - SOP-HR-002: Employee Onboarding & Verification (reference document).

5. CORE WORKING HOURS AND AVAILABILITY MANDATES
   All remote employees must abide by the following operational availability rules:
   5.1. Daily Core Schedule
        - All remote employees must be active, online, and accessible during the
          mandatory collaborative core hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST.
        - Exceptions must be approved in writing by the Team Manager 24 hours in
          advance, or logged under formal leave policies.
   5.2. Daily standup Attendance
        - Attendance at the daily department standup is mandatory. Standup timings 
          are fixed by the team lead and must occur within core hours.
   5.3. Communication Latency limits
        - Response times on direct company messaging tools (Slack, Teams) during
          core working hours must not exceed 60 minutes.
        - If an employee is offline or in a meeting, they must update their status 
          icon and calendar accordingly.

6. SECURE HOME WORKSPACE PROTOCOLS
   Employees are responsible for ensuring their home office environment is secure:
   6.1. Private Connection
        - Remote work must be done over the company-provided VPN.
        - Open public Wi-Fi networks are strictly forbidden.
   6.2. Visual Privacy
        - Monitors displaying customer PII or engineering codebase logs must not
          be visible to third parties, including family members.

7. ERGONOMIC HOME OFFICE EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
   PulseGuard provides financial support for setting up a healthy remote workspace:
   7.1. Funding Limits
        - Remote employees are eligible for up to $500 total reimbursement for 
          approved home office ergonomic chairs, desks, and external monitors.
   7.2. Receipt Submission Rules
        - To qualify for reimbursement, receipts must be submitted via the HR Portal
          within 30 days of purchase.
        - Submissions made 31 days or more post-purchase will be rejected.

8. PROCEDURAL STEP-BY-STEP FLOW
   Step 1: Employee applies for remote-work status through the HR portal.
   Step 2: Manager approves remote status based on performance.
   Step 3: Employee purchases ergonomic chair (under $500 limit).
   Step 4: Employee logs receipt in HR reimbursement system within 30 days.
   Step 5: HR Coordinator reviews request and checks timestamp.
   Step 6: Finance issues reimbursement on the next pay cycle.
   Step 7: Employee maintains 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM availability and VPN security daily.

9. TROUBLESHOOTING & ESCALATION PROCEDURES
   9.1. Responsiveness Failures
        - If an employee fails to respond within 60 minutes multiple times, the
          manager will log a performance incident and notify HR.
   9.2. Escalation Contact Path
        - First Level: Department Team Manager.
        - Second Level: HR Director (Kavita Sharma).
        - Executive Escalation: VP of Engineering (Sneha Deshmukh).
